Manual test cases:
1. Start app and choose 1 to list certificates (should show sample data inserted).
2. Choose 2 to add certificate, verify appears in list.
3. Choose 5 or 6 to verify certificates by id or serial.
4. Choose 3 to update an entry and confirm changes.
5. Choose 4 to delete an entry and verify removal.
6. Choose 7 to demo searching and sorting (LINQ/Array).

Edge cases:
- Verify non-existent id/serial returns NotFound.
- Expired and revoked certificates should return appropriate statuses.
